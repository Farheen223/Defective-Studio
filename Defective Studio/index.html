Introduction Tutorial

Make Your Own Game. Everything You Need. Get Started Now. In this 10-part video course, you’ll learn how to make your first game from scratch with Defective Studio. We’re supplying you with all the art, sound and tutorials you need. Our goal in this course is to help you create a game that could potentially be featured by Apple or reach the top the charts. To accomplish this, you have to make your game unique. There are hundreds of games released on a regular basis across every single platform. This makes originality crucial for not only visibility in the App Store but overall player engagement.

In this first lesson, a lot of information will be covered to provide you with a solid foundation to work with. We’ll explain exactly what Buildbox is and how you can use it to build your own game without using a single line of code. will help you become more familiar with the features and editing tools that you’ll be using. You’ll learn how to use the options available in the collision shape editor to resize, add points, and reset an image. This video will introduce you to scratch and then you click on the tutorial button in the homepage and find more videos as it is guided you on how to make one.
